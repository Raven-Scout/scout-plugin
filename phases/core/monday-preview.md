## Monday Preview

**Applies only on `weekend-briefing` runs** (the Run Modes table routes here; skip it on a weekday `morning-briefing`).

Give {{USER_NAME}} a head start on the next workday. After the calendar query, look ahead to the **next workday** (Monday, or the next non-holiday weekday) and surface:

- **Meetings** on the next workday — count, the first meeting's time, and any that need prep that isn't already written.
- **Imminent deadlines** — action items or tracked tasks due on or shortly after the next workday, so nothing lands cold on Monday morning.
- **Anything time-sensitive** queued over the weekend that will need attention first thing.

Keep it short — a preview, not a full briefing. The goal is "here's what Monday looks like," not a second action-items pass. Fold it into the weekend wrap notification and the Scout Digest rather than emitting a separate message.
